# Team Communication Script — Roadmap Announcement (Jun 13-14)

## Context

**Situation:** After 6 months of -52.3% revenue decline (EUR 973k → EUR 464k projected), team morale is at risk. Finance is tight, uncertain about future.

**Message:** "We're not collapsing. Here's a concrete 6-month plan to recover and grow."

**Audience:** Full team (~6-8 people: engineers, support, finance)

**Format:** In-person meeting (30-45 min) + written summary

**Timing:** ASAP (Jun 13-14), before Madeira outreach begins Jun 15

---

## Part 1: Context Setting (5 min)

**Tone:** Honest, transparent, solutions-focused

> "First, let me be clear: 2026 has been challenging. We lost a major client (30% of revenue) in January. That hurt. Revenue dropped 52%. I won't sugarcoat it.
> 
> But here's what I want you to know: **This is fixable.** And I have a concrete plan.
> 
> I spent the last week analyzing what happened, talking to remaining clients, researching new markets. That work resulted in a strategy that I believe in.
> 
> Today I want to share that with you — not the executive summary, the **real plan** — with timelines, targets, and what each of you will be doing."

---

## Part 2: Financial Reality (7 min)

**Show data (print out or share screen):**

```
2025: EUR 973k (baseline)
2026 H1: EUR 232k (Jan-Jun) — client lost
2026 projection (no change): EUR 464k (-52%)

Root cause: NIF 511099177 client dropped from EUR 288k → EUR 11.6k (-96%)
Margin: 52% (stable) — NOT a cost problem, a revenue problem
```

**Key insight to land:**

> "Our business model is healthy. 52% margin means we can invest in growth. The problem isn't that we're broken — it's that one client left, and we didn't replace them.
> 
> That was my job. I failed. But I'm fixing it now."

---

## Part 3: The Plan (10 min)

### Strategic Direction: "Two paths in parallel"

**Path 1: Recover & Retain (Defensive)**
- Try to recover NIF 511099177 client (EUR 200k potential)
- Keep Top-5 remaining clients happy (EUR 100-150k annual)
- Launch Suite integration (EUR 20-50k from cross-sell)

**Path 2: Grow (Offensive)**
- Madeira GTM campaign (EUR 150-400k new revenue)
- 2027 Continente expansion (EUR 1M+ target for next year)

**Result:** EUR 350-450k in 2026 (sustainable), EUR 1M+ in 2027

---

### Timeline & Responsibilities

**June (This month):**
- FA: Hire sales contractor (Madeira outreach support)
- FA: Try recovery call on lost client
- Dev: Continue SvelteKit PWA (Aug 31 target)
- All: Stand by for launch

**July-August:**
- FA + Contractor: Run Madeira demos (target 3-5 scheduled)
- Dev: Finish PWA + record demo video
- FA: QBRs with Top-5 clients (Suite pitch)
- Support: Prepare for Madeira onboarding

**September-December:**
- FA + Contractor: Close 3-5 Madeira logos (EUR 150-400k)
- Ops: Prepare 2027 hiring (Lisbon sales rep)
- Dev: Suite Phase 2 (webhook + billing)
- All: Reflect on 2026, plan 2027

---

## Part 4: What This Means For You (10 min)

### For Engineers

> "SvelteKit PWA is critical. Madeira demos need a working product. 
> 
> Target: Aug 31 live. That means:
> - Jul: 80% done
> - Ago 15: 95% done
> - Ago 31: Launch
> 
> I know it's aggressive. It's also doable — we've built 90% of this already.
> 
> If you hit this, I'll buy you a week off in September. Seriously."

**Action items:**
- [ ] Prioritize PWA over other features
- [ ] Weekly status sync with FA (Mondays)
- [ ] Test Demo mode (demoData.ts with 8 devices, 10 events)

### For Support/Operations

> "You'll support Madeira onboarding. That means:
> - 2-3 new customer integrations (Jul-Sep)
> - Onboarding training (2-3 days per client)
> - QBR prep for existing clients
> 
> This is your chance to shape how we support customers. Do it well, and you'll own Customer Success when we hire someone in 2027.
> 
> Plus: If you help close a deal, you get 5% referral commission. (So if FA brings in EUR 50k, that's EUR 2.5k for you.)"

**Action items:**
- [ ] Document onboarding playbook (Madeira template)
- [ ] Prepare 3 SLA tiers (Good/Medium/Low)
- [ ] Create Madeira-specific FAQ

### For FA (Me)

> "I'm going 30h/week on GTM, 10h/week on product. That means:
> - Hiring a contractor ASAP (sales/marketing)
> - Partnering with consultancies (referral channels)
> - Running Madeira demos + closes
> - Keeping Top-5 clients happy (QBRs)
> 
> It's a lot. But it's what needs to happen."

---

## Part 5: Compensation & Growth (5 min)

> "I know money's been tight. Let me be transparent about 2026 salaries:
> 
> - If we hit EUR 350k (realistic): Everyone keeps current salary + EUR 2k bonus (Sep-Dec)
> - If we hit EUR 450k (optimistic): Everyone gets 10% raise + EUR 5k bonus
> - If we hit EUR 550k (stretch): Everyone gets 15% raise + EUR 8k bonus
> 
> For 2027, if we hire for Lisbon / expansion:
> - Anyone who helped close a Madeira deal: first right of refusal for team lead / sales operations role
> - Equity: Top 2 performers get 0.1-0.2% founder shares (cliff 1yr, vest 4yr)
> 
> This isn't guaranteed — it's tied to hitting targets. But it's real."

---

## Part 6: Risk Management (3 min)

> "Be honest: What could go wrong?
> 
> **Worst case:** Madeira campaign fails, we're stuck at EUR 350k (no growth)
> → Plan B: Contract model (consultancy partnerships do sales for us)
> 
> **If top client churns:** We're down to EUR 250k
> → Plan B: Extend payment terms, reduce salary increase timing
> 
> **If SvelteKit PWA slips to Sept:** Demos delayed 1 month
> → Plan B: Run demos on old UI, upgrade in-product
> 
> **If I get hit by a bus:** 😄
> → Plan B: Operations manager takes over (TBD who that is)
> 
> I'm not hiding risks. I'm naming them, so we can prepare."

---

## Part 7: Call to Action (5 min)

**Final message:**

> "I'm asking three things from you:
> 
> **1. Believe in the plan.** I know 2026 has been rough. But this is concrete, achievable, and I've stress-tested it. I believe in it.
> 
> **2. Commit to your part.** Devs: Aug 31 PWA. Support: onboarding playbook. Me: EUR 350-450k by Dec. No excuses.
> 
> **3. Call me out if I'm wrong.** If you see risks I'm missing, or opportunities I'm not taking, speak up. I'm not always right. But I'm always listening.
> 
> We built JFA Unify together. We're going to grow it together. Jun 15 we start executing.
> 
> Questions?"

---

## Part 8: Q&A Likely Questions (Prep answers)

### Q: "Why are we focused on Madeira instead of recovering the big client?"

**Answer:**
> "Good question. Two reasons:
> 
> 1. We don't have contact info for that client (lost in email, no current contract)
> 2. Even if we find them, success is uncertain (might've switched vendors)
> 
> Madeira is addressable (40+ leads, market growing, no strong incumbents). Statistically, we have better odds closing 3-5 new customers than recovering 1 lost one.
> 
> If we find the old client's contact by Jun 15, we'll absolutely try recovery. But I'm not betting the company on it."

### Q: "Are salaries safe? Are we laying anyone off?"

**Answer:**
> "Salaries are safe. I'd cut my own salary before cutting anyone else's. 
> 
> We have ~3 months of runway at current burn rate. Madeira campaign + existing clients should get us to breakeven by Sep. From there, we grow.
> 
> If something catastrophic happens (I lose the remaining Top-5 clients), we'd need to do a reset. But I'm not expecting that. Plan assumes we keep them."

### Q: "What if Madeira doesn't work?"

**Answer:**
> "Then Plan B is contractor sales model. We partner with 3-4 consultancies, they do sales for commission, we do implementation. Lower risk, lower upside.
> 
> But I think Madeira works. I've talked to 5 hotel owners, 2 school directors. Interest is real. They're just not aware of us yet."

### Q: "What about the NIS2 / CNPD compliance stuff? Should we be pushing that as a selling point?"

**Answer:**
> "Great question. Yes, we should. Compliance is a tail wind (every regulated entity needs to show access logs). 
> 
> Support + Ops team: Can you draft 1-page 'CNPD Compliance via JFA Unify' doc? That goes into cold emails + demos.
> 
> It's not the primary selling point (cost is), but it's a strong secondary."

### Q: "Is there a bonus if we hit targets?"

**Answer:**
> "Yes. I mentioned it earlier: EUR 2-5k per person + potential 10-15% raise + equity for top performers.
> 
> This is real money, contingent on hitting EUR 350k+ by Dec. It's in writing in the bonus policy I'm distributing today."
