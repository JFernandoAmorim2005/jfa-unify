## Interview Process

### Round 1: Phone screen (30 min)
**Conducted by:** Fernando Amorim
**Topics:**
- Why JFA Unify? Why Lisbon? Why now?
- Previous sales roles: quota, product, team size
- Motivations: base salary vs equity upside? Growth trajectory?

**Pass criteria:** Enthusiasm + relevant experience + cultural fit

### Round 2: Sales exercise (45 min)
**Conducted by:** Fernando Amorim
**Task:** Role-play JFA Unify demo for a hotel owner
- Candidate pitches 5-min demo (we provide materials)
- Q&A from "hotel owner" (Fernando asks hard questions)
- Close attempt (identify objections, overcome, ask for deal)

**Pass criteria:** Clear communication, handles objections, consultative approach

### Round 3: References + due diligence (1 week)
**Call 2 previous sales managers:**
- "Tell us about this person's sales ability"
- "Why did they leave?"
- "What would be their biggest challenge in our role?"

**Background check:** Standard PT checks (criminal, credit, employment verification)

### Round 4: Offer + negotiation
**If passed:** Offer letter with base/commission/equity breakdown
**Negotiation:** Open to equity increase (up to 1.5%) if candidate has exceptional track record

## Onboarding Plan (4 weeks, Jan 2027)

**Week 1: Product immersion**
- Product walkthrough (full demo)
- Architecture deep-dive (database, API, MQTT)
- Customer success stories (Madeira closes, if available)
- Hands-on: Set up test tenant, create devices, run queries

**Week 2: Market research**
- Competitor landscape (Securitas, Tyco, local players)
- Lisbon market analysis (hotel count, school count, office buildings)
- Customer personas: ideal profile for each segment
- Pricing justification (cost of ownership vs traditional systems)

**Week 3: Sales skills transfer**
- Sales playbook walkthrough (docs/sales-pitch, objection handlers)
- CRM training (setup, pipeline stages, forecasting)
- Email templates + cold outreach methodology
- Demo delivery practice (3+ full runs with feedback)

**Week 4: First campaigns**
- Build prospect list (40+ initial targets)
- Send first batch of cold emails
- Schedule 2-3 exploratory calls (non-sales, information gathering)
- Review performance with Fernando

**Ongoing (monthly):**
- Weekly 1-on-1 with Fernando (pipeline review, coaching)
- Monthly all-hands (product updates, market feedback)
- Quarterly business review (targets, compensation, growth)
