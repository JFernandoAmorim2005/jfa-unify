# Expansion Continente — 2027 Launch Strategic Plan

## Markets

### Priority 1: Lisbon (Metro Area)
- **Why:** Largest market in Portugal, enterprise density, partnerships available
- **Segments:** Hotels (4-5 star), Office buildings, Corporate HQs
- **Population:** ~3M metro area
- **Target:** 5-10 logos in Y1

### Priority 2: Porto (Metropolitan Area)
- **Why:** Secondary market, growing tech/startup hub
- **Segments:** Hotels, schools, tech offices
- **Target:** 3-5 logos in Y1

### Priority 3: Algarve (Regional Hub)
- **Why:** Tourism hub, many hotels and resorts
- **Segments:** Hotels, tourism infrastructure
- **Target:** 3-5 logos in Y1

---

## Go-to-Market (2027 Roadmap)

### Phase 1: Lisbon Base (Q1 2027)

**Timeline:** Jan 1 - Mar 31, 2027

**Resources:**
- Hire 1 sales rep (local, PT-speaking, B2B enterprise experience)
- Establish office space or hot-desk in Lisbon
- Partner with 2-3 security consultancies (commission 15%)

**Activities:**
- Week 1-2: Onboard sales rep, prepare materials (in Portuguese, PT context)
- Week 3-4: Partner meetings + sign agreements
- Week 5+: Begin outreach (40+ leads per month)

**Target by Mar 31:**
- 2-3 demos booked
- 1+ proposal sent

### Phase 2: Porto + Algarve Expansion (Q2-Q3 2027)

**Timeline:** Apr 1 - Sep 30, 2027

**Resources:**
- Expand sales to 2-3 reps total (or commission-based partners)
- 5-6 consultancy partnerships
- Possible regional office (Porto or Algarve)

**Target by Sep 30:**
- 5-10 new logos (Lisbon + Porto + Algarve)
- EUR 400-600k revenue run-rate

### Phase 3: Scale (Q4 2027 onwards)

**Timeline:** Oct 1 - Dec 31, 2027+

**Full sales team:**
- 3-4 account executives
- 1 customer success manager
- 1 operations/admin
- Marketing budget (LinkedIn, events, referrals)

**Target by Dec 31, 2027:**
- 15-25 total customers (Madeira + Continente)
- EUR 1.0-1.5M annual revenue
- EUR 80k+ monthly recurring revenue

## Contingency Plans

**If Lisbon slower than expected:**
- Shift budget to consultancy commissions (higher % to drive referrals)
- Accelerate Porto/Algarve
- Consider hiring contractor sales rep (vs. full-time) if cashflow tight

**If competition intense:**
- Emphasize local support + integration story
- Build case studies in Madeira for proof-of-concept
- Partner with more consultancies (5-6 instead of 2-3)

**If staffing challenge:**
- Outsource sales to recruitment firm (commission-based)
- Build partner channel (consultancies do sales, we do implementation)
